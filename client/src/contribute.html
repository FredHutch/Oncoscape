Decoding Cancer

Join us in creating the next generation diagnostic platform

Oncoscape is a web-based data exploration and visualization portal.

Our goal is to equip both researchers and doctors alike with the necessary tools and resources to discover, develop, and implement first class treatment strategies to revolutionize personalized patient care.

Get involved

The bottleneck in cancer research used to be our ability to generate and collect data. With high-throughput technologies becoming more cost effective, integrating and interpreting data is now the most time consuming step. We want your help in expediting the discovery process. If you are a software developer, UX architect, graphic designer, data scientist, or even part time programmer looking to make a difference, we would love to hear from you. A background in biology is not necessary. We have needs ranging from the design of webpages and updating user documentation to developing new machine learning methods. Join us in making a difference.

Our Community

Fred Hutch along with industry partners GitHub and Amazon want to connect with you. As stated by Fred Hutch’s Director of Human Biology Eric Holland, MD, PhD, “The most exciting thing about software is that it opens up science to everyone. Programmers can help bring the vision of Oncoscape to life, ultimately helping doctors access data in a way that lets them make better clinical decisions.” Make your impact by joining us in this crowd coding effort.
